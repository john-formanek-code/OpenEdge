# Code of Conduct

## Our standards
- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent.
- Focus feedback on ideas and implementation, not people.

## Unacceptable behavior
- Harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks.
- Doxxing or privacy violations.
- Disruptive trolling or spam.

## Enforcement
Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject content that violates this code and may limit participation for repeated violations.
